# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 24, 2026

**The Dump** is operated by George Labs ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Account information:** Email address (used for authentication via Firebase)
- **Notes and content:** Text notes, voice memos (audio files), photos, and uploaded documents/files
- **Subscription information:** Plan type, billing status (payment details are handled by Apple or Stripe â€” we don't store your credit card number)

### Data Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** Feature usage, note counts, timestamps
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Crash and performance data:** Error logs to help us fix bugs

## 2. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---|---|
| Provide the service (store and retrieve your notes) | Notes, audio, photos, files |
| AI-powered organization and categorization | Note content (text, transcribed audio) |
| Account authentication | Email address |
| Billing and subscription management | Subscription status |
| Improve the App | Aggregated usage and performance data |
| Communicate with you | Email address |

**We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your personal data. Ever.**

## 3. How AI Processes Your Content

Your notes are processed server-side by AI to automatically organize and categorize them. Specifically:

- Text notes and transcribed voice memos are sent to our servers for analysis.
- We use third-party AI services (currently **OpenAI** and/or **Anthropic**) to help with categorization and semantic understanding.
- These AI providers process your content under their data processing agreements with us. Per our agreements, they **do not use your content to train their models**.
- AI processing is used solely to provide you with the organization features of The Dump.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Firebase (Google)** | Authentication, infrastructure | Email address, account identifiers |
| **OpenAI** | AI content processing | Note content (text) |
| **Anthropic** | AI content processing | Note content (text) |
| **Apple** | In-App Purchase billing | Subscription/transaction data (managed by Apple) |
| **Stripe** | Web subscription billing | Email, payment info (managed by Stripe) |

Each provider operates under its own privacy policy and our data processing agreements.

## 5. Data Storage & Retention

- Your notes, audio files, photos, and documents are stored on our servers for as long as your account is active.
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest.
- When you delete a note, it is removed from our active systems. Backup copies may persist for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.
- When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your content for any purpose other than providing the service

We **may** disclose data if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

## 7. Your Rights

### All Users
- **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data through the App's settings, or by contacting us.
- **Portability:** Request an export of your notes in a standard format.
- **Correction:** Update your account information at any time.

### California Residents (CCPA)
Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell it, so there's nothing to opt out of)
- Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at support@georgelabs.ai.

### European Economic Area Residents (GDPR)
If you're in the EEA, our legal bases for processing your data are:
- **Contract performance:** Processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
- **Legitimate interest:** Improving the App, ensuring security
- **Consent:** Where required (e.g., optional communications)

You also have the right to:
- Restrict or object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
- Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is the basis for processing)

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at support@georgelabs.ai.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Dump is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we've collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us at support@georgelabs.ai.

## 9. Data Security

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. When data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with applicable law.
